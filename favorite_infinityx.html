Favorite Things

I derived great satisfaction from my engagement in this project, as it afforded me the opportunity to exercise my creativity by implementing a concept I had envisioned while integrating elements reminiscent of my preferred websites.

Reusable Gallery And Gallery Card Component

I thoroughly enjoyed the project's opportunity to incorporate a snap-scroll gallery feature using a reusable gallery card React component. This component was successfully employed across spacecraft, spaceport, and planet galleries. Furthermore, the flexibility of the gallery card component allowed for rendering cards based on the specific gallery type being viewed.

Consistent Redux Store Shape

One aspect I particularly enjoyed about this project was the strategic design of my Redux store structure. By organizing it into slices that have a maximum depth of 2 levels, I effectively mitigated issues related to stale store references during store updates and ensured the desired component re-renders.

Outer Space Theme

One captivating aspect of this project is the opportunity it provided to delve into an outer space theme during the application development process. It allowed me to thoroughly enjoy creatively implementing features for hypothetical scenarios that transcend earthly boundaries!
