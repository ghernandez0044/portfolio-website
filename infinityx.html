Overview

It is developed using Python, SQL, SQLAlchemy, Flask, PostgreSQL, React, and Redux. I followed an Agile Methodology, prioritizing one feature at a time and completing its end-to-end functionality before moving on. This approach ensures efficient development and a seamless progression from backend to frontend.
